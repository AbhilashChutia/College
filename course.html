Our Courses

Intermediate

The University of Assam provides multiple Intermediate Course. This includes courses under the curriculum on Science, Arts and Commerce. The staff are world class professors that are profecient in all curriculum topics and are experts in lab practice. The laboratories in our universitiy are state of art with well practiced lab assistants. Alumni of these courses are well to do scientists, engineers, journalists

Ungergraduate

The Undergraduate courses include bachelors' in Science with majors in Mathematics, Geology, Physics, Computer Science, Political Science, Chemistry etc. These courses are well defined and includes very proficient professors who take their job very seriously. We have a 100% placement rate for all out undergraduate students who are selected from campus interviews from top of the world Information technology comnpanies.

Post Graduation

The Post Graduation courses include masters degree courses in Computer Science, Engineering, Geology, mathematics, Bio Chemistry, Mass Communication, Applied Physics , Astro Physics and many more. Post Graduates students are given the opportunity to intern in doctorate level laboratories and data science labs. These students get very profecient in applied work and are usually known to get very high paying placements from in-campus interviews.
